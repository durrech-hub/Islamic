About the teacher

Slow lessons, kept privately

I teach one student or one small halaqah at a time — never a crowded class where questions get skipped for the sake of pace. Every session is recorded for the student to keep, and every learning plan is rebuilt around where you actually are, not a fixed syllabus.

Sessions run in English and Arabic, and I teach children from age seven through adult learners returning to the deen later in life.
